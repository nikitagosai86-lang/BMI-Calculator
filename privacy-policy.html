Privacy Policy – BMI Calculator

BMI Calculator respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Our app does not collect any personal data, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location

The app only uses the following inputs:

  • Height
  • Weight
  • Age
  • Gender
This information is used only for BMI calculation and is not stored, shared, or transmitted.

2. Data Usage

  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No data is sent to any server
  • We do not track or store user activity

3. Data Security

We value your trust:

  • No personal data is stored online
  • All data remains on your devices
